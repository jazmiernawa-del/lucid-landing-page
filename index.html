The truth about caffeine

It was never the caffeine.
It was the amount.

For years we were told caffeine was the enemy. Cut it out, switch to decaf, feel guilty about the second cup. But that's not actually the problem — the problem is what most coffee does with it: 150, 200, sometimes 300mg dumped into your system at once, no buffer, no balance.

LUCID flips that. 90mg is enough to wake you up. It's not enough to wreck your afternoon. Paired with L-theanine and lion's mane, the lift comes in smoother and stays longer, instead of hitting hard and leaving fast.

You don't need to fear caffeine. You just need less of it, done right.
